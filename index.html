About Me

I am now an Assistant Professor at the Medical School of Nanjing University. From March 2019 to April 2023, I was an Associate Researcher at the School of Electrical Science and Technology of Nanjing University and a member of CITE Lab.

I am currently working with Prof. Ning Gu and Prof. Xun Cao. My research interests include Computational Optics and Microscopy, especially focusing on designing novel and miniaturized microscopes (on-chip microscope, light-field microscope, and micro-endoscope) and developing iterative/intelligent algorithms based on physical imaging processes (3D fluorescent imaging and 3D echocardiogram). For students who are interested in our research area, welcome to join us.
